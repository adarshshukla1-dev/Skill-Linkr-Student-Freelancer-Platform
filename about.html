About Skill Linkr

Empowering Students. Supporting Clients. Building Futures.

Our Story

Skill Linkr was founded with a simple idea — to give talented students a fair chance to earn, grow, and prove their skills through real-world projects. Many students struggle to find opportunities despite having the right talent, and small businesses or startups struggle to afford skilled freelancers.

Skill Linkr bridges that gap by connecting student freelancers directly with clients at minimal prices — creating a win-win ecosystem for both.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To create a platform where every student can showcase their skills, earn income, and gain real experience while studying.

🚀 Vision

To become India’s largest student-powered freelance community — where learning meets earning.

Meet Our Founder

Adarsh Shukla

Adarsh is a passionate Computer Science student, entrepreneur, and founder of Skill Linkr. His mission is to empower students like himself to gain confidence, financial independence, and professional growth through freelancing.

He believes that true education happens when skills meet opportunities — and Skill Linkr is his step toward that vision.
